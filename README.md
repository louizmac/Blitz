<h1 align="center">Blitz</h1>

<p align="center">
  <strong>A lightning-fast, floating quick-note app for macOS.</strong>
</p>

<hr>

<h2>⚡️ The Problem</h2>
<p>Switching contexts to write down a quick thought, idea, or to-do breaks your focus. Opening a full-featured notes app takes too long and disrupts your current workflow.</p>

<h2>✨ The Solution</h2>
<p><strong>Blitz</strong> provides a floating note panel that appears instantly over any app. Whenever an idea strikes, hit a global shortcut, type it out, and dismiss the window without ever leaving your workspace.</p>

<table width="100%">
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h3>🚀 Global Shortcut</h3>
      <p>Toggle the floating note panel from absolutely anywhere by pressing <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>L</kbd>.</p>
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h3>⚡️ Quick Organization</h3>
      <p>Instantly capture thoughts, add titles, color-code your notes, and search through them in a clean, scrollable list.</p>
    </td>
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h3>🎨 Native Design</h3>
      <p>Built natively with SwiftUI and AppKit. It features a stunning glassmorphic UI with smooth animations that feel like part of the OS.</p>
    </td>
    <td width="50%" valign="top">
      <h3>🏗️ Menu Bar Resident</h3>
      <p>Blitz lives quietly in your menu bar. No Dock clutter, no distractions. Just there when you need it.</p>
    </td>
  </tr>
</table>

<h2>🛠 Installation</h2>
<ol>
  <li><strong>Download</strong> the latest <code>.app</code> from the Releases page.</li>
  <li><strong>Move</strong> <code>Blitz.app</code> to your <code>/Applications</code> folder.</li>
  <li><strong>Launch</strong> the app. It will appear in your Menu Bar.</li>
  <li><strong>Grant Permissions:</strong> If prompted, ensure the app has Accessibility permissions so the global keyboard shortcut can work system-wide.</li>
</ol>

<blockquote>
  <p><strong>Tip:</strong> Add Blitz to your <strong>Login Items</strong> in System Settings so it's always ready when you start your Mac.</p>
</blockquote>

<h2>🕹 Usage</h2>
<ol>
  <li><strong>Press</strong> <kbd>Cmd</kbd> + <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>L</kbd> from anywhere to summon the Blitz shelf.</li>
  <li><strong>Click</strong> the <kbd>+</kbd> icon to create a new quick note.</li>
  <li><strong>Type</strong> out your idea, give it a title, and assign it a color category.</li>
  <li><strong>Drag</strong> the window to move it wherever it's most convenient.</li>
  <li><strong>Press</strong> the shortcut again (or click away) to hide the panel. Your notes auto-save in the background.</li>
  <li><strong>Copy</strong> your notes instantly to the clipboard with one click, or right-click to delete them.</li>
</ol>
